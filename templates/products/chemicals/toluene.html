Toluene

High-strength aromatic solvent delivering excellent solvency and consistent industrial results.

Toluene is an aromatic hydrocarbon solvent widely used for its strong dissolving power and compatibility with various industrial formulations. It is a clear, water-insoluble liquid with a characteristic aromatic Odor.

Advantages

  • Strong Solvent Power
  • Good Chemical Stability
  • Excellent Compatibility
  • Enhances Coating Flow & Finish
  • Cost-Effective Industrial Solvent
  • Reliable Performance

Applications

  • Paints, Coatings & Thinners
  • Printing Inks
  • Adhesives & Rubber Industry
  • Chemical Manufacturing
  • Fuel Blending Components
