Mix Xylene

Powerful multi-purpose solvent for superior thinning, cleaning, and coating performance.

Download Datasheet

PDF • Updated 2026

Mix Xylene (Mixed Xylenes) is a versatile aromatic hydrocarbon solvent widely used in industrial applications. It is a blend of different isomers of Xylene and offers excellent solvency, fast evaporation, and strong compatibility with various chemicals and resins.

Advantages

  • Excellent Solvent Power
  • Fast Drying Properties (Fast Evaporation)
  • High Compatibility with Paints & Coatings
  • Effective Cleaning & Degreasing Agent
  • Stable Chemical Properties
  • Cost-Effective Industrial Solvent
  • Good Resin Solubility
  • Enhances Coating Performance

Applications

  • Paints, Coatings & Thinners
  • Printing Inks & Adhesives
  • Rubber & Leather Processings
  • Chemical Manufacturing
  • Cleaning & Degreasing Applications
  • Agrochemical Formulations
  • Petrochemical Industries

Performace Highlights

  • Clear & Bright Appearance
  • High Purity (Min 98%)
  • Consistent Distillation Range
  • Strong Solvency Power
  • Fast Evaporation Rate
  • Low Water Content
  • Reliable Industrial Performance
