Phenol

Essential industrial chemical for versatile manufacturing and synthesis applications.

Phenol is a widely used aromatic organic compound known for its strong chemical reactivity and versatility in industrial applications. It is a colorless to light pink crystalline solid with excellent properties for use in chemical synthesis and manufacturing processes.

Advantages

  • High Chemical Reactivity
  • Excellent Intermediate for Chemical Synthesis
  • Widely Used Across Multiple Industries
  • Good Solubility in Organic Systems
  • Reliable & Consistent Industrial Performance
  • Essential Raw Material for Resin Production

Applications

  • Phenolic Resins Manufacturing
  • Plastics & Polymer Industry
  • Pharmaceuticals & Disinfectants
  • Adhesives & Laminates
  • Chemical Intermediates Production

Performace Highlights

  • High Purity (Typically 99%+)
  • Strong Chemical Reactivity
  • Moderate Melting Point (~40–41°C)
  • High Boiling Point (~182°C)
  • Colorless to Light Pink Appearance
