Acetone

Ultra-fast evaporating solvent delivering powerful cleaning and high-performance industrial efficiency.

Acetone is a fast-evaporating, highly effective solvent widely used in industrial and commercial applications. It is a colourless, volatile liquid known for its excellent solvency and quick drying properties.

Advantages

  • High Solvent Power (Excellent solvency)
  • Very Fast Evaporation
  • Leaves No Residue
  • High Purity & Consistency
  • Effective Cleaning Agent
  • Wide Industrial Compatibility

Applications

  • Paints, Coatings & Thinners
  • Nail Polish Removers & Cosmetics
  • Adhesives & Sealants
  • Cleaning & Degreasing
  • Pharmaceutical & Chemical Processing

Performace Highlights

  • High Purity (Min 99.5%)
  • Ultra-Fast Evaporation
  • Low Boiling Point
  • Strong Cleaning Efficiency
  • Clear & Bright Appearance
