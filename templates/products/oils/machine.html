Machine Oil

Reliable lubrication engineered for smooth machine performance and extended equipment life.

Download Datasheet

PDF • Updated 2026

Machine Oil is a high-quality general-purpose lubricant formulated to provide effective lubrication, wear protection, and smooth operation of industrial machinery. Designed for consistent performance, it ensures reduced friction, protection against rust and corrosion, and improved operational efficiency.

Its balanced viscosity and thermal stability make it suitable for a wide range of light to medium-duty industrial applications.

Technical Specifications

Analytical Test Machine Oil

Appearance Clear & Bright
Density @29.5°C 0.860 – 0.880

Flash Point (COC) Min 200°C
