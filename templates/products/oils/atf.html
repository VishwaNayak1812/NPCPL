Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF)

Engineered transmission fluid for extreme operating environments.

Download Datasheet

PDF • Updated 2026

Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF) is a high-performance lubricant specially formulated for automatic transmissions. It ensures smooth gear shifting, excellent thermal stability, wear protection, and extended transmission life under demanding operating conditions.

Available Grades:

  • ATF Type A
  • ATF Dexron III
  • ATF Dexron VI

Advantages

  • Smooth & Seamless Gear Shifting
  • High Thermal & Oxidation Stability
  • Superior Wear Protection
  • Excellent Friction Control
  • Improved Fuel Efficiency
  • Extended Drain Intervals
  • Outstanding Low-Temperature Flow
  • High Flash Point for Safety
  • Enhanced Transmission Durability

Applications

  • Passenger Cars with Automatic Transmission
  • SUVs & Light Commercial Vehicles
  • Heavy Duty Vehicles (where applicable grade is recommended)
  • Power Steering Systems
  • Hydraulic Systems requiring ATF specification
  • Modern Transmission Systems requiring Dexron III / Dexron VI

Performance Highlights
