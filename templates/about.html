Our Journey of Excellence

Newage Petro and Conchem Pvt. Ltd. (NPCPL), established in 1994 (formerly known as Newage Industrial Oils Pvt. Ltd.), is a trusted name in the manufacturing, import, export, and supply of industrial oils, heavy aromatic solvents, and specialty chemicals for the oil and gas sector.

With over three decades of industry presence, NPCPL has built a reputation rooted in integrity, technical excellence, and long-term partnerships. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Girish Bhojak, the company has steadily evolved into a dependable and performance-driven organization serving both domestic and international markets.

Headquartered in Vadodara, Gujarat (India), our manufacturing facility is equipped with modern infrastructure, advanced processing systems, and stringent quality control mechanisms. Our operations are aligned with recognized certifications and industry standards, ensuring that every product delivered meets uncompromising benchmarks of safety, consistency, and performance.
